About Me

My name is Jonah Kane. I am a brand new rookie web developer with very limited experience but I have a whole lot of drive and desire to learn. I decided to get into this crazy world becuase I lost my job. I spent the last 5 years of my professional career working as a Dispatch Manager for a local ready-mix concrete supplier. I wanted a job that would be fun yet challenging - but most important - I wanted a job that would be in high demand. Something that might make looking for work a little bit easier. So I decided to "go back to school."
